Fluffy Pancakes

Here’s a quick recipe for fluffy pancakes:

Ingredients: 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour, 3 ½ tsp baking powder, 1 tsp salt, 1 tbsp sugar, 1 ¼ cups milk, 1 egg, 3 tbsp melted butter (plus extra for cooking)

Instructions: 1. In a bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. 2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and melted butter. 3. Pour wet ingredients into the dry and stir until just combined (don’t overmix). 4. Heat a lightly buttered skillet over medium heat. 5. Pour ¼ cup batter per pancake; cook until bubbles form, then flip. 6. Cook until golden on both sides.

Serve warm with syrup or toppings of your choice!

Classic Omelette

Here’s a quick classic omelette recipe:

Ingredients: 2-3 eggs, 2 tbsp milk or water, Salt and pepper (to taste), 1 tbsp butter, Optional fillings: cheese, ham, veggies, etc.

Instructions: 1. In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk (or water), salt, and pepper. 2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat until melted. 3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and let it cook without stirring. 4. Tilt the pan as edges set, letting uncooked egg flow to the edges. 5. Add desired fillings on one side. 6. Fold the omelette in half and slide onto a plate.

Serve hot!

French Toast

French Toast Recipe:

Ingredients: 2 eggs, ½ cup milk, 1 tsp vanilla extract, 1 tsp cinnamon, 4 slices of bread, Butter for cooking

Instructions: 1. Whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon in a bowl. 2. Dip bread slices into the mixture, coating both sides. 3. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat. 4. Cook each bread slice until golden brown on both sides.

Serve with syrup or powdered sugar.

Avocado Toast

Avocado Toast Recipe:

Ingredients: 1 ripe avocado, 2 slices of bread, Salt, pepper, and chili flakes (to taste), Lemon juice

Instructions: 1. Toast the bread until crisp. 2. Mash the avocado with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice. 3. Spread the mashed avocado on the toasted bread. 4. Sprinkle chili flakes for extra flavor.

Chole Bhature

For Chole:

Ingredients: 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight), 1 onion (chopped), 2 tomatoes (pureed), Spices: garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, cumin, Oil

Instructions: 1. Pressure cook chickpeas until soft. 2. Sauté onion in oil, add spices and tomato puree. 3. Cook until oil separates, then mix in chickpeas and simmer.

For Bhature:

Ingredients: 2 cups flour, ½ cup yogurt, A pinch of baking soda, Salt, Oil (for deep frying)

Instructions: 1. Knead flour, yogurt, baking soda, and salt into a soft dough. Rest for 1-2 hours. 2. Roll into circles and deep fry until puffed and golden.

Serve Chole with hot Bhature.
